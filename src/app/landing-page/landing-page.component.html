Discover the Advantages


The growing acceptance and consequent rise in total installed capacity of solar PV systems across the globe is a testament to the numerous advantages of solar. To this end the gospel of solar cannot be over emphasized.

ENVIRONMENTALLY FRIENDLY

Aside the very low levels of environmental pollution attached to the manufacturing process of PV panels, PV systems are considered pollution free during operation. That is, solar panels do not release harmful air or gasses into the atmosphere, or endanger animal or human health.

CHEAPER LIFETIME COST

Though the initial setup cost of solar is high compared to other sources of energy, the lifetime cost of solar is cheaper compared to fossil fuels and other renewable sources. The breakeven point is usually between 5-10 years, depending on geographical region, government policy and a few other factors.

LESS MAINTENANCE REQUIRED

There’s really less maintenance required with solar, mainly due to the fact that there are no moving parts. The main maintenance required is cleaning, which can be done a couple of times in a year.

DIVERSE APPLICATION

Solar energy can be harnessed for electricity generation and heating of homes. Solar PV systems can be deployed for electricity generation of varying scale; utility-scale, commercial-scale or small scale.

However, solar PV systems are being used in countries across the world including Nigeria to achieve access to electricity in rural areas not connected to the national grid or where connection to the national grid is a lot more expensive than using solar.

All Roads Lead to Solar!


Do not be left in the dark! The gains of going green are too enormous to be ignored.

Remiedating The Enviroment:

The use of fossil fuels for electricity generation and other mining activities causes the release of toxic GHG gases into the atmosphere which is a major cause of climate change. To this end continuous use of fossil fuels for energy generation is strongly being campaigned against in order to keep the earth healthy for human habitation in the near future.

Solar PV systems lead the way in the global campaign for a sustainable, reliable and healthy alternative to fossil fuels.

A Noiseless Alternative To The Noisy Generators

To cap its very low maintenance requirement compared to petrol and diesel generators, solar power systems are absolutely noiseless, non-pollutant and pose no health risks during operation. You and your loved ones can have a sound and safe night rest without fear of being choked by exhaust fumes, as well as the deafening noise made by these generators.

Exist In Abundance

Unlike fossil fuels with limited deposits in few geographical regions of the world, the sun is widely available in all regions of the world and is expected to remain so for as long as the earth exists. The fear of depletion associated with fossil fuels and pollution that accompany their transportation and exploration is eliminated by harnessing the sun’s energy.

Ever Rising Electricity Tariffs

The rising cost of electricity tariffs is not peculiar to Nigeria but a global trend and it’s forecast to maintain an upward climb. With solar PV solutions you can significantly reduce the cost on electricity bills while also meeting the energy needs of your home/business.
