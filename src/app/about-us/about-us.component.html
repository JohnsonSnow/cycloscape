Our Company Overview

We are a renewable energy company (with particular focus on solar PV systems) headquartered in Lagos. We offer affordable and reliable products and services in virtually all segments of the solar PV market; sales, design and installation, training and O&M across Nigeria.

Design and installation

We at Cycloscape energy boast of our expertise in the design and installation of reliable and very efficient solar power solutions not just because of our technical qualifications and dexterity but also because we've had our own fair share of frustrations and failures from poorly designed and installed systems. From installing systems completely vulnerable to lightning strikes, systems that just stand as a mere home ornament on overcast(cloudy) days to grossly misinforming a few clients on capabilities of their peculiar system…to mention a few.

Today these experiences and lessons learned have formed a vital part of our strength. Leveraging on our pool of experience and knowledge, use of sophisticated softwares and hardwares, an amazing teamwork and high quality system components we deliver a solar solution that adequately meets your home/office energy need, a system well secured and protected, a system optimized for maximum energy generation possible on a cloudy day.

Training

Stemming from the findings of our R&D team on the standard of installation being done in Nigeria, we’ve come to acknowledge that there’s a shortage of finely skilled solar power installers in the country. And that the quality of service rendered by some of these poorly skilled installers have amplified the erroneous belief held by some that solar is not viable, economical, reliable as being touted globally. This to an extent has hampered the deep penetration/acceptance of solar in Nigeria.

Again, given the rapid pace of growth in the global solar market owing to improved technology and Sharp fall in prices of solar panels and other system components, there’s an increasing number of solar companies springing up in Nigeria in reaction to the global trends and advocacies on solar power as well as the growing acceptance of solar power solutions in Nigeria. It is evident that the solar industry in the country is starved of finely skilled manpower, despite there being a teeming population of unemployed youths.

Due to these keen observations, we at Cycloscape decided as part of our social-corporate responsibility to equip the youths of this country desiring to dabble into the solar landscape in whatever capacity (freelance installers, sales/marketing employees, entrepreneurs) with the right skill set.

Cycloscape solar academy is opened to individuals and groups (corporate firms) wanting to be skilled or re-skilled in the solar power sector.
